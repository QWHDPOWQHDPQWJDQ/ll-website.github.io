> Information

Why did we choose Vlogging? We choose vlogging for several different reasons:


  • Behind-the-Scenes Access
  • Human Connection
  • Engagement
  • Visual Storytelling

    • Why did we vlog our journey? The reason we vlog our journey is because we'd like to reach our target audience and to show them why we do what we do.

    > Target Audience

    Our target audience consists of teens and adults, our age range is around 13-20.


    Why did we choose our target audience? One of the reasons we choose teens is because they're usually on the internet and they also have friends and family that they can vlog with. Even if they don't have income, they have the ability to spread the word about Sitio Bebe's situation.
