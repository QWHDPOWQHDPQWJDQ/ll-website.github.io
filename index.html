> Information

Why did we choose Vlogging? We choose vlogging for several different reasons:


  • Behind-the-Scenes Access
  • Human Connection
  • Engagement
  • Visual Storytelling

    • Why did we vlog our journey? The reason we vlog our journey is because we'd like to reach our target audience and to show them why we do what we do.

    > Target Audience

    Teen Demographic

  • Teens
  • Age: 13 - 20
  • Employment: Unemployed, ususally in high school
  • Education: Highschool
  • Income: Low, usually supported by the family

    • Why did we choose our target audience? One of the reasons we choose teens is because they're usually on the internet and they also have friends and family that they can vlog with. Even if they don't have income, they have the ability to spread the word about Sitio Bebe's situation.


    Adult Demographic

  • Adults
  • Age: 20
  • Employment: Employed
  • Education: Highschool or College
  • Income: Mid to high
  • People in this target range are employed

    • We chose adults as one of the target audiences because they can talk to their friends or colleagues about our concert fundraiser. And because they have the income to donate to our fundraiser.

    > Needs

  • Microphone
  • Camera
  • Good Camera Skills
  • Computer

    • > Summary

    Our vlogs intend to enlighten the viewer on the serious health crisis currently happening in Sitio Bebe, showing the lives of the many who live in Sitio bebe and their issues. It will also document the process of making our fundraiser event in order to give the viewer an idea of the inner workings of the event. And lastly our vlog serves to inform the viewer on how their donation will greatly help Sitio Bebe’s community’s current health crisis.
